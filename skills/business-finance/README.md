## Available Skills

### [US Tax Compliance for Foreign Entities](./us-tax-compliance-foreign-entities/)
Expert guidance on C-Corp and LLC tax compliance in the United States for foreign owners and non-resident aliens. Covers filing requirements, deadlines, tax elections, withholding obligations, and optimization strategies.

**Main resources:**
- [SKILL.md](./us-tax-compliance-foreign-entities/SKILL.md) - Complete federal tax compliance guide
- [Wyoming Guide](./us-tax-compliance-foreign-entities/wyoming-guide.md) - State-specific formation and compliance ($60/year, best for privacy)
- [Delaware Guide](./us-tax-compliance-foreign-entities/delaware-guide.md) - State-specific formation and compliance (best for VC-backed startups)
- [UPDATES.md](./us-tax-compliance-foreign-entities/UPDATES.md) - Research sources and version history

**Use cases:**
- Setting up US entities as a foreign national (Wyoming vs Delaware decision)
- Understanding IRS filing requirements for foreign-owned businesses
- Navigating tax treaties and withholding obligations
- Optimizing entity structure for tax efficiency
- Ensuring compliance with FinCEN and FBAR requirements
- Choosing the right state for incorporation (cost vs funding considerations)
