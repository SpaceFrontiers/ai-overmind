### Key Updates Made

#### 1. Corporate Transparency Act (CTA) - 2024 Requirements
**Updated Information**:
- Effective date: January 1, 2024
- Deadlines verified:
  - Pre-2024 entities: Deadline was January 1, 2025
  - 2024 formations: 90 days
  - 2025+ formations: **30 days** (shortened deadline confirmed)
  - Updates: 30 days after any change
- Penalties confirmed: $500/day up to $10,000 civil, plus criminal penalties
- Filing portal: FinCEN BOI reporting system

**Sources**: 
- "The Corporate Transparency Act: A New Reporting Requirement Affects Many Agricultural Businesses" (2024)
- Telegram business news channels (Russian-language legal updates)

#### 2. Tax Rates - TCJA Provisions (Verified Current)
**Confirmed Rates**:
- C-Corp federal rate: **21%** (flat rate since January 1, 2018)
- FDAP withholding: **30%** statutory (or reduced treaty rate)
- Section 1446 withholding: **37%** (individuals), **21%** (corporations)
- Individual ECI: 10-37% graduated rates

**Sources**:
- "Corporate tax in the United States" (Wikipedia, updated 2025)
- "Cut Your Client's Tax Bill: Individual Planning Tips and Strategies" (2020)
- "(Un)intended consequences? The impact of the 2017 tax cuts and jobs act" (Academic study)

#### 3. Section 1446 Withholding - Partnership Rules
**Updated Details**:
- Withholding applies to allocable share of ECI (whether distributed or not)
- Rates: Highest marginal rates (37% individual, 21% corporate)
- Tax treaties do NOT reduce Section 1446 rates
- Quarterly installments via Form 8813 required
- Form 8804 due April 15, Form 8805 due March 15

**Sources**:
- "International Taxation" by Adnan Islam (2020)
- "Aspen Treatise for Introduction To United States International Taxation" (2021)
- IRS regulations Sections 875 and 1446

#### 4. Form 5472 Penalties - Increased
**Updated Information**:
- Penalty increased from $10,000 to **$25,000** per form
- Additional $25,000 per month of continued failure
- Applies to:
  - 25%+ foreign-owned US corporations
  - Foreign-owned disregarded entities (single-member LLCs)

**Sources**:
- "International Accounting" by Doupnik, Finn, Gotti (2023)
- "International Aspects of the US Taxation System" by Lessambo (2016)
- Multiple academic sources confirming penalty increase

#### 5. FDAP Income - 30% Withholding
**Confirmed Information**:
- Fixed, Determinable, Annual, Periodic income taxed at 30%
- Includes: dividends, interest, rents, royalties
- Reduced by tax treaties (typically 0-15% for dividends, 0-10% for interest/royalties)
- Withholding agent becomes personally liable if fails to withhold
- Forms 1042 and 1042-S required for reporting

**Sources**:
- "International Aspects of the US Taxation System" by Lessambo (2016)
- "International Taxation" (2020)
- Multiple tax treaty references

#### 6. Bonus Depreciation Phase-Down
**New Information Added**:
- 100% bonus depreciation (2018-2022)
- 80% in 2023
- 60% in 2024
- 40% in 2025
- Phases down further in subsequent years

**Source**: Academic analysis of TCJA provisions

#### 7. Section 163(j) Interest Deduction Limits
**Clarified Rules**:
- Limitation: 30% of adjusted taxable income
- 2018-2021: Based on EBITDA (more generous)
- 2022 onward: Based on EBIT (more restrictive)
- Designed to prevent overleveraging and tax avoidance

**Source**: TCJA impact studies and tax reform analysis
