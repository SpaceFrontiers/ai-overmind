## Key Tax Rates (2024-2025)

**Federal**:
- C-Corporation: 21% (flat rate)
- FDAP withholding: 30% (or treaty rate)
- Section 1446 withholding: 37% (individuals), 21% (corporations)
- Individual ECI: 10-37% (graduated)

**State**:
- Wyoming: $60/year (LLC), no income tax
- Delaware: $300/year (LLC), $450-$200k/year (Corp), no income tax on out-of-state income

**Penalties**:
- Form 5472 late filing: $25,000 per form
- BOI late filing: $500/day (up to $10,000)

## Critical Deadlines

**One-Time (Formation)**:
- BOI Report to FinCEN: **30 days** after formation (2025 onwards)
- EIN from IRS: Apply immediately
- Operating Agreement/Bylaws: Within first month

**Annual**:
- Wyoming Annual Report: First day of anniversary month ($60)
- Delaware Franchise Tax: March 1 ($450+ for corps, $300 for LLCs)
- Federal Returns: March 15 (partnerships), April 15 (corporations, individuals)

## Wyoming vs Delaware: Quick Comparison

| Feature | Wyoming | Delaware |
|---------|---------|----------|
| **Annual Cost (LLC)** | $60 | $300 |
| **Annual Cost (Corp)** | $60-$200 | $450-$200,000 |
| **Privacy** | Excellent (members not public) | Good (shareholders not public, officers are) |
| **Asset Protection** | Strongest (charging order) | Standard |
| **VC Preference** | Not preferred | Required by 95%+ |
| **Best For** | Privacy, costs, asset protection | VC funding, IPO, tech startups |

## Common Use Cases

### Case 1: E-Commerce Business (No VC Funding)
**Recommendation**: Wyoming LLC
- Annual cost: ~$185 ($60 Wyoming + $125 registered agent)
- Privacy: Member names not public
- Asset protection: Strongest charging order protection
- Federal: File Form 1040-NR + Form 5472

### Case 2: SaaS Startup (Seeking Seed Funding)
**Recommendation**: Delaware C-Corp
- Annual cost: ~$625 ($450 franchise tax + $125 agent + $50 report)
- VC requirement: Almost all VCs require Delaware
- Fundraising: Standard cap table, stock options, preferred stock
- Federal: File Form 1120

### Case 3: Consulting Business (Foreign Owner, No Employees)
**Recommendation**: Wyoming LLC (single-member)
- Annual cost: ~$185
- Tax: Pass-through to owner (Form 1040-NR)
- Simple structure, minimal compliance
- Federal: Form 1040-NR + pro forma 1120 + Form 5472

### Case 4: Holding Company (Own Intellectual Property)
**Recommendation**: Wyoming LLC
- Annual cost: ~$185
- Privacy: Ownership not disclosed
- Asset protection: Strong charging order protection
- Tax: Pass-through or C-Corp election available
